What is Uplus?

Uplus is an app that allows you to collectively contribute with funds to social events together with your friends and family.You could for example use it to save money together for an upcoming birthday or wedding. You can also use the app to directly transfer money to other people!

What is the purpose of Uplus

Our purpose is to simplify collective funding for Rwandans! Typically, people spend a lot of time on collecting money physicallyor from different mobile money accounts. This can be a real hazzle and it is tedious to keep track on who collected with how much. Wewant to change this and make it easy -so you can spend your time on more fun things!

How does it work?

By downloading the app and creating an account, you can easily invite your friends and create a group. By connecting your MTNaccount or credit card, you can start saving together and make direct transfers. For the group functionality, each groups selects a trusted treasurer by voting. The treasurer is the only person who can authorize withdrawals –this way, not anyone withdraw money and leave.

Do i need a smartphone?

Yes, for the moment you need a smartphone. It can be an Android or an iPhone. Currently, other smartphones (e.g. Windows) arenot supported –but we are working on it.

Is my money safe with you?

Yes, it is. As we collaborate with MTN Mobile money and Bank of Kigali, the money is stored at separate account at our partners as soon as you make a transaction. The safety of your money is our number one concern!

Does it cost anything?

Currently, Uplus is free of charge. However, as any other company Uplus needs to make profit in order to survive long term. In the future, there might be some fees related to our services.

How can I transfer money via Uplus?

You simply add your credit card or connect your MTN mobile money account to the app and then you can start making transactions.

Do you accept Mastercard, VISA, Airtel Momo and MTN Momo?

Currently only Mastercard and MTN mobile money works. We are working on adding VISA and Airtel money as our collaborating partners –it will come very soon!

What are the benefits of Uplus?

There are multiple benefits with Uplus, here are a few:

1. Easy to use -o need to manually collect money from you friends, it is all in the app! Smooth and easy with a few clicks in the app!

2. Time saver-It gathers all the transaction information for you in one place so you can quickly overview everything!

3. Safe–Your money is safe with us thanks to our partners: MTN mobile money and bank of Kigali.

4. Quick–Money is transferred instantly so it goes quick to collect money for your events!

Do all members in a Uplus group need a smartphone?

Yes, for now all members need a smartphone! We are working on a USSD solution.
